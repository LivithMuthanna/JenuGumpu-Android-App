**Jenu-Gumpu** (Kannada for "Honey Collective") is a mission-driven Android application designed to empower tribal and rural honey hunters in Karnataka. By bridging the gap between forest-dwelling producers and urban markets, the app replaces exploitative middleman cycles with a verified, data-driven "Forest-to-Table" ecosystem.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🍯 Intelligent Harvest Management
*   **Full CRUD Lifecycle**: Log harvests with precision, including floral source (Wildflower, Coffee Blossom, etc.), quantity, and location.
*   **Traceability**: Automated batch ID generation ensures every jar of honey can be traced back to its forest origin.

### 📈 Market Transparency (Live Price Index)
*   **Retail vs. Wholesale**: Real-time monitoring of urban retail prices vs. local wholesale rates.
*   **Interactive Analytics**: Custom-built line charts (using Jetpack Compose Canvas) to visualize market trends and regional bullish/bearish sentiments.

### 🧠 Jenu-Bee: The AI Assistant
*   **Powered by Gemini Pro**: An AI companion that provides sustainable harvesting advice, quality grading tips, and purity analysis guidance.
*   **Expert Q&A**: Real-time support for honey producers to resolve quality issues on the fly.

### 💰 ROI Journey (Profit Manifesto)
*   **Value Optimization**: A specialized calculator that shows producers how processing (filtering, moisture control, branding) transforms raw liquid value into premium market yields.

### 🌍 Hyper-Local Experience
*   **Fully Bilingual**: Native support for **Kannada** and **English**, including numerical localization.
*   **Adaptive Design**: High-contrast UI with Light and Dark modes, optimized for outdoor use in diverse lighting conditions.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

-   **Language**: Kotlin
-   **UI Framework**: Jetpack Compose (Modern, declarative UI components)
-   **Backend**: 
    -   **Firebase Firestore**: Real-time NoSQL database for cloud synchronization.
    -   **Firebase Auth**: Secure identity management via Google Login.
-   **AI Integration**: Google Generative AI SDK (Gemini Pro).
-   **Architecture**: MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel) for scalable and maintainable code.
-   **Asynchrony**: Kotlin Coroutines & Flow for reactive state management.

## 🚀 How It Works

1.  **Harvest**: A hunter logs their collection in the forest.
2.  **Grade**: The hunter uses the visual reference and AI advice to classify their honey (Grade A+ to C).
3.  **Monitor**: Before selling, the hunter checks the Price Monitor to see current retail values in urban centers.
4.  **Collective Sync**: Every entry is synced to the cloud, allowing the "Collective Hub" to see total stock weight for better bargaining.
5.  **Manifest Profit**: Use the ROI calculator to see exactly how much profit is gained after purification and packaging.

## 📈 Impact & Vision

In many tribal regions, hunters sell 1kg of premium wild honey for prices significantly lower than the urban retail value. **Jenu-Gumpu** changes the narrative by:
*   **Eliminating Information Asymmetry**: Giving producers the same data as buyers.
*   **Ensuring Sustainability**: AI education on preserving bee colonies and protecting forest ecosystems.
*   **Building Brand Identity**: Moving from "anonymous raw liquid" to "certified premium forest honey."
